Details & FAQ

Michael & Stephanie are getting married at West Avenue Cider House on Saturday, September 7th, 2019 at 5:30 PM ET with dinner and drinks to follow.

West Avenue Cider House is a farm, orchard, and cidery located in Freelton, ON. The ceremony and reception are both taking place outdoors so be sure to dress for the weather (there will be a tented area for the reception & dinner).

Some Frequently Asked Questions are answered below:

Where should I park?
There is plenty of (free!) parking at the venue. It will be on your right as you're pulling in, near the cider house.
Are children allowed?
We would appreciate it if you could find a sitter for the evening (and let's face it, you deserve it).
Do you have a registry?
Yes, we are registered with Hudsons Bay. Click here to take a look.
Do I still have a question?
If you do, please reach out to us: wedding@dukich.online
